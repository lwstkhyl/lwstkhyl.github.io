### 文献阅读

[Integrated multimodal cell atlas of Alzheimer’s disease](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41593-024-01774-5)
- 全球首个阿尔茨海默病多模态细胞图谱

[Integrating human endogenous retroviruses into transcriptome-wide association studies highlights novel risk factors for major psychiatric conditions](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-024-48153-z)
- 如果HERV在一个基因内部（同义链），且基因本身也表达，则有可能是构成了这个基因转录本的一个Isoform; 如果该基因表达低、HERV在反义链、或HERV周围没有基因，则更可能是它自己的转录

[Somatic cancer variants enriched in Alzheimer’s disease microglia-like cells drive inflammatory and proliferative states](https://www.cell.com/cell/fulltext/S0092-8674(26)00341-7)
- 这些microglia-like cells （小胶质样脑巨噬细胞）跟你在mic里看到的亚组有关嘛

[stellarscope原文：A single-cell transposable element atlas of human cell identity](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12296447/)
- 用hERV定义细胞类型

[Single-cell eQTL mapping of human endogenous retroviruses reveals cell type-specific genetic regulation in autoimmune diseases](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-025-62779-7)
- 分析非常全面 可以效仿 就是生物学上差点意思

[A retrotransposon caught red-handed in a curious case of missing digits](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41588-025-02147-9)
